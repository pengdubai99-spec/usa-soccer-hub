## 🛠️ 1. Critical Technical Setup (Avoiding Bans & Targeting the US)
Meta's security algorithms are extremely aggressive against new accounts created via VPNs. If not careful, your accounts will be flagged as bots.

### 🔒 Account Warm-Up & Proxy Guide
1. **VPN vs. Residential Proxies:**
   - **Avoid free VPNs.** Their IP addresses are blacklisted by Meta.
   - **Recommendation:** Use a high-quality paid VPN with a **Dedicated/Static IP** (e.g., Surfshark, NordVPN) or, ideally, a **US Residential Proxy** (e.g., Webshare, Proxy-Cheap) configured via a browser anti-detect tool (like AdsPower or Multilogin) or post from a mobile device properly set up.
2. **The 3-Day Warm-Up Rule:**
   - **Do not post immediately.**
   - Spend the first 2-3 days behaving like a normal user: Search for official FIFA, USMNT (US Men's National Team), and MLS accounts. Like a few posts, watch some Reels, and follow 5-10 major accounts.
   - Do not spam follow or comment.
3. **Mobile Device Settings (For Reels Posting):**
   - If posting from a phone:
     - Remove your local SIM card (or disable the SIM profile). Meta checks SIM country codes.
     - Use a US eSIM (if possible, even a data-only one) or stay entirely on Wi-Fi with your VPN/Proxy on.
     - Change your phone's system language to **English (US)** and timezone to a US zone (e.g., EST/PST).
     - Turn off Location Services (GPS) for Instagram/Facebook.

## 🚫 2. Copyright & Content Safety (The "Anti-Ban" Rules)
FIFA is notoriously aggressive with copyright strikes. If you post actual match footage, your page will be **banned within hours**.

### ❌ Do NOT Post:
- Live match video clips.
- Official broadcast highlights (Fox Sports, Telemundo, BBC, etc.).
- Official FIFA theme songs/soundtracks without permission (unless using them via Instagram's official music library).

###  Post Instead (Safe Content):
- **Fan-Generated Content:** Crowd reactions, stadium atmosphere, fan vlogs (always credit the original creator in the caption: *"Cc: @creator"*).
- **AI-Generated Match Previews & Predictions:** Use visual graphics showing face-offs (e.g., USA vs England) with prediction percentages.
- **Match Stats & Player Spotlights:** High-quality graphics showing player stats, head-to-head comparisons, and key matchups.
- **Interactive Trivia & Memes:** Quizzes, historical World Cup facts, and trending soccer memes.
- **News & Daily Roundups:** Text-on-screen videos summarizing daily news using copyright-free background photos/videos.

*   **Strategy:** Write quick articles like "How to watch USA vs. [Opponent] live in the US", "USA World Cup Squad List", or match analysis.
*   **Traffic:** Put the link in your bio and direct your social media followers to it: *"Full schedule and streaming links in bio!"*
*   **Monetization:** Put Google AdSense or Ezoic ads on the site. Sports traffic during the World Cup is extremely high and CPC (Cost Per Click) in the US is very lucrative.

*   **Requirements:** 
    - **Instagram/Facebook Reels Play:** Invite-only, requires US Tax ID (W-9) if registering as a US creator.

*   *Note:* If you cannot provide a US Tax ID, you can monetize through your local country's program once you meet the requirements, or partner with someone in the US, or set up a US LLC later if the page grows massive.
