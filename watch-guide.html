How to Watch USA vs Paraguay Live: TV Channels & Free Stream Options

The wait is finally over! The United States Men's National Team (USMNT) takes to the pitch for their highly anticipated 2026 FIFA World Cup opening group stage match against Paraguay. As the co-hosts of the tournament, expectation has never been higher for the Stars and Stripes to make a statement in their opening game.

If you're in the United States and looking to capture every pass, tackle, and goal live, here is the complete breakdown of official TV networks, kick-off times, and free streaming trial platforms.

USA vs Paraguay Kickoff Date & Time

The match is scheduled to take place at the iconic SoFi Stadium in Los Angeles, California.

⏰ Match Details
  • Date: Friday, June 12, 2026
  • Kick-off Time: 7:00 PM EST / 4:00 PM PST
  • Venue: SoFi Stadium, Los Angeles (CA)
  • Group Stage: Group D - Matchday 1

How to Watch USA vs Paraguay on Television (Cable TV)

For traditional TV viewers, the match will be broadcast in both English and Spanish nationwide:

  • English-Language Broadcast: The game will air live on FOX. Pre-game coverage begins one hour prior to kickoff.
  • Spanish-Language Broadcast: The game will air live on Telemundo and Universo.

How to Live Stream USA vs Paraguay in the US (No Cable Required)

If you are a cord-cutter, you can stream the entire match live in high definition through several subscription and free-trial streaming services:

1. Peacock (Best Budget Option)

Peacock will stream every single match of the 2026 FIFA World Cup live, with professional Spanish-language commentary. This is the cheapest way to watch the games officially online. Plan starts at $5.99/month, and you can cancel anytime.

Stream on Peacock

2. FuboTV (Free Trial Option)

FuboTV is an excellent streaming choice for soccer fans. It carries both FOX (English) and Telemundo (Spanish) in its base channel package. Fubo offers a 7-day free trial for new users, which allows you to watch the USA vs Paraguay match completely free of charge. Be sure to cancel before the trial ends if you don't wish to keep the subscription.

Watch Free on FuboTV

3. Sling TV, YouTube TV & Hulu + Live TV

Other major streaming services that carry FOX and FS1 include:

  • YouTube TV: Offers a complete package including local FOX affiliates and Telemundo, with unlimited cloud DVR space. Free trials are typically available.
  • Sling TV (Blue Package): Provides a cost-effective way to get Fox Sports (in select local markets) and FS1.
